## 🔄 4-Week Technical Core Rotation (June 15 - July 5, 2026)

Once the non-tech lectures are completed and Compiler Design is consolidated, we transition to a systematic rotation of core CS and IT-specific subjects.

```
Week 2: DBMS & Digital Logic ➔ Week 3: OS & COA ➔ Week 4: CN & TOC ➔ Week 5: Algorithms & IT-Specifics
```

### Week 2 (June 15 - June 21): DBMS & Digital Logic
* **DBMS Focus**: ER Model, Relational Model, SQL Queries (HAVING, JOINs, IS NULL), Normalization (1NF, 2NF, 3NF, BCNF), Transactions & Concurrency Control (Strict 2PL, Thomas' Write Rule).
* **Digital Logic Focus**: Boolean Algebra, K-Maps, Combinational Circuits (Multiplexers, Full Adders), Sequential Circuits (Flip-Flops, Counters).
* **Non-Tech**: General Awareness & Current Affairs (last 6 months) + Coal Sector GK.

### Week 3 (June 22 - June 28): Operating Systems & COA
* **Operating Systems Focus**: Processes & Threads, CPU Scheduling, Synchronization (Semaphores, Monitors), Deadlocks, Memory Management (Paging, Segmentations, Virtual Memory, EMAT), File Systems, Disk Scheduling.
* **COA Focus**: Pipelining (Hazards, Speedup, Clock Cycles), Cache Memory Address Mapping (Direct, Associative, Set-Associative), Instruction Formats, Addressing Modes.
* **Non-Tech**: Daily 30-minute Aptitude/Reasoning practice on the walking pad.

### Week 4 (June 29 - July 5): Computer Networks & TOC
* **Computer Networks Focus**: OSI & TCP/IP layers, Routing Protocols (Dijkstra, Distance Vector), Flow Control (Sliding Window), Congestion Control, IP Addressing (Classful, CIDR, Subnetting), TCP/UDP, Network Security.
* **TOC Focus**: Finite Automata (DFA, NFA, Regular Expressions), Context-Free Grammars (CFG, PDA), Chomsky Hierarchy (Type-0, 1, 2, 3), Turing Machines, Decidability.
* **Non-Tech**: English Grammar & Vocabulary drills + Coal Sector GK.

### Week 5 (July 6 - July 12): Algorithms & IT-Specifics
* **Algorithms Focus**: Asymptotic Analysis, Greedy Algorithms, Dynamic Programming, Divide and Conquer, Graph Algorithms (Dijkstra, Bellman-Ford, Kruskal, Prim, Floyd-Warshall).
* **IT-Specifics Focus**: 
  * **Cyber Law in India**: IT Act 2000 (Sections 66A, 66C, 66D, 66E, 67, 43), Cert-In, Digital Signatures.
  * **Software Engineering**: SDLC Models (Waterfall, Spiral, Agile), COCOMO Model formulas, Testing (Black-box, White-box).
  * **Linux System Administration**: Basic commands (`chmod`, `chown`, `grep`, `find`, `tar`), permissions, and file system hierarchy.
