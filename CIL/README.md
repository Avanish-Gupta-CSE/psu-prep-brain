## 🚨 CIL MT (Systems) Overview

* **Designation**: Management Trainee (Systems) — E-1 Grade
* **Pay Scale**: ₹60,000 – ₹1,80,000 (Basic pay of ₹60,000 during 1-year training, then absorbed as Officer)
* **Vacancies**: **43 Total** (OBC-NCL: 11, UR: 19, SC: 6, ST: 3, EWS: 4)
* **Selection Process**: **Computer Based Test (CBT) Only — NO INTERVIEW!** Selection is based purely on CBT merit.
* **Target**: **AIR 1** (Absolute merit list dominance).

## 📚 Resource Hierarchy & Course Mapping

To guarantee conceptual depth and exam-oriented speed, we will use a multi-layered resource strategy:

```
[GO Classes] ➔ [YourPedia Recorded/Live] ➔ [Active Practice & PYQs]
(Deep Clarity)     (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)     (Walking Pad Revision)
```

1. **GO Classes (Primary for Core Tech)**:
   * Use for deep, detailed GATE-level conceptual clarity on core subjects (DBMS, OS, CN, TOC, Compiler, Algorithms, DS).
   * If any YourPedia live or recorded lecture lacks clarity, immediately pivot to GO Classes to patch the gap.
2. **YourPedia CIL Course (Primary for Exam Orientation & Non-Tech)**:
   * **Live Lectures**: Attend/watch active live lectures (currently **Compiler Design**).
   * **Recorded Lectures (2024/2025)**: Use as a backup for pre-studying subjects before they are taught live, or as a secondary reference if a live teacher's explanation is unsatisfactory.
   * **Non-Tech Classes**: Watch Aptitude, Reasoning, and English classes at 1.5x/2x speed using the **Rule of Active Testing** to compress coverage.
3. **Active Practice & PYQs (Primary for Speed & Retention)**:
   * Practice GATE and PSU previous year questions (PYQs) on your walking pad to stay active, absorb anxiety, and build rapid question-solving reflexes.

## 🛡️ Core Rules for Success

1. **PS5 Weekday Quarantine (Rule R-I)**: Unplug all PS5 cables and store them in your office bag. Zero weekday gaming. Weekend gaming strictly capped at 1 hour max, only after gym and study blocks are complete.
2. **Post-Gaming Reset (Rule R-J)**: Never go to bed or sleep immediately after gaming. Complete a 15-minute walk or take a room-temperature shower first to stabilize your brain's dopamine baseline.
3. **Fasted Morning Block**: Wake up at 5:00 AM, take Thyroxine 50 mcg fasted, and immediately execute a 3-hour fasted study block (Core Tech) before the gym.
4. **Active Non-Tech Testing**: Never watch non-tech lectures passively. Test yourself first; only watch lectures for topics where your accuracy falls below 80%.
