# MSTC — Mission & Vision (Official)

Source: [VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx)

## Vision

- To be the global market leader in e-commerce domain. ([VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx))
- To emerge as a dominant player in secured and transparent trading. ([VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx))
- Creating value from waste resources through sustainable and eco-friendly recycling. ([VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx))

## Mission

- To ensure transparency and better price discovery through extensive use of e-commerce. ([VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx))
- To ensure hassle-free and fair e-commerce enabled trading. ([VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx))
- To promote sustainable and eco-friendly recycling. ([VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx))
- To strive for continuous innovation to deliver desired value to our stakeholders. ([VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx))
- To penetrate and expand the markets we handle and enhance the value of services we render on sustained basis. ([VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx))

## Objectives

- To increase India’s share in global cross border trade by facilitating reliable e-commerce enabled trading. ([VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx))
- To improve customer experience and make a significant positive impact on customers' satisfaction by providing prompt and efficient services to business associates, driving improved loyalty to its business. ([VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx))
- To provide a secure and transparent e-commerce platform enabling better price discovery and meet the transactional requirements of Indian and cross border public and private sector enterprises. ([VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx))
- To develop and maintain a competent, dedicated and motivated workforce. ([VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx))
- To, enter into joint ventures with enterprises offering synergy in the area of metal and e-waste recycling, prospective business on e-commerce platform. ([VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx))
- To undertake these activities so as to ensure an optimum return on capital employed and to attain a return of 15% on the net worth. ([VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx))
- To build demand and increase supply of recycled metal and e-waste commodities in the country by investing in recycling capacity building and providing a transparent platform for organized disposal of scrap. ([VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx))
