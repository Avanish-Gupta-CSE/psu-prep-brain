# MSTC VMO — Mnemonics (Study Aid)

**Confirmed source text:** [VMO | MSTC Limited](https://mstcindia.co.in/content/vmo.aspx)

This file is a memorization layer (mnemonics). Use it to **recall keywords in order**, then paraphrase naturally in the interview.

## 1) VISION — 3 points

**Mnemonic: `G-D-R` → “Global Dominant Recycler”**

- **G — Global leader** in e-commerce domain
- **D — Dominant player** in secured & transparent trading
- **R — Recycling value** from waste (sustainable & eco-friendly)

Say (paraphrase): “Vision is to be a global e-commerce leader, a dominant force in secure transparent trading, and create value from waste through sustainable recycling.”

## 2) MISSION — 5 points

**Mnemonic: `T-H-P-I-P` → “THIPP”**

- **T — Transparency** & better price discovery via e-commerce
- **H — Hassle-free** & fair e-commerce enabled trading
- **P — Promote** sustainable & eco-friendly recycling
- **I — Innovation** continuously to deliver stakeholder value
- **P — Penetrate & expand** markets; enhance service value sustainably

Say (paraphrase): “Ensure transparency and price discovery, ensure hassle-free fair trading, promote sustainable recycling, strive for continuous innovation, and penetrate/expand markets.”

## 3) OBJECTIVES — 7 points

**Mnemonic: `I-C-P-W-J-R-B` → “Indian Customers Prefer World-class JVs for Returns & Build”**

### Chunk 1 — Market (I-C-P)

- **I — Increase** India’s share in global cross-border trade
- **C — Customer** experience & satisfaction improve (prompt + efficient services)
- **P — Provide** secure, transparent platform for price discovery & transactions

### Chunk 2 — People & Partners (W-J)

- **W — Workforce** competent, dedicated, motivated
- **J — Joint ventures** in metal & e-waste recycling + synergy businesses

### Chunk 3 — Money & Material (R-B)

- **R — Return** optimum on capital; target return of **15% on net worth**
- **B — Build** demand/supply of recycled metal & e-waste via capacity building + transparent disposal platform

## 2-minute daily drill

1. Write: **`GDR – THIPP – ICPWJRB`**
2. Expand each once (aloud) in under 2 minutes
3. In mocks: do not quote exact lines; say **2–3 keywords per point** with confidence
