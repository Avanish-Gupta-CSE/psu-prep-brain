# Interview Plan

## Objective

Stay interview-ready without spending so much time here that HPCL suffers.

## Warm-Maintenance Plan

1. Build and refine the self-introduction
2. Build MSTC-specific `why company / why PSU / why you` answers
3. Convert resume bullets into interview stories
4. Prepare behavioral answers for ownership, pressure, conflict, and learning

## 48-Hour Drill If Interview Call Arrives

### Block 1
- Re-read `.brain/MSTC-GD-Prep.md`
- Revise MSTC business verticals, recent initiatives, financials, and digital platforms

### Block 2
- Rehearse self-introduction, `why MSTC`, `why private to PSU`, `why should we hire you`

### Block 3
- Rehearse 8 to 10 career stories from `CAREER-STORY-BANK.md`

### Block 4
- Run one full mock HR interview and one mixed HR + behavioral interview

## Interview Mix To Expect

- HR motivation and career decisions
- Company-fit and PSU motivation
- Resume walkthrough
- Behavioral / leadership-style questions
- Light technical or role-fit follow-ups based on current projects
